## 🔥 SERP Domination Strategy

### Ranking Tactics

#### 1. Competitor Comparison Pages
Target queries: `[Provider A] vs [Provider B]`, `best FM provider UK`

**Implemented:**
- `/compare/entirefm-vs-mitie` - Direct head-to-head comparison
- Feature comparison tables
- Client testimonial positioning
- CTA: "Request Comparison Proposal"

**To Create:**
- EntireFM vs CBRE
- EntireFM vs ISS UK
- EntireFM vs OCS
- Best FM Provider for [Sector]

#### 2. Authority Articles (Featured Snippet Targets)
These capture 99% of FM research queries:

**Implemented:**
- `/resources/top-fm-providers-uk` - Rankings & methodology
- `/resources/fire-alarm-testing-frequency` - BS 5839 compliance guide

**To Create:**
- "How to switch FM provider stress-free" (guide format)
- "FM compliance checklist 2025" (downloadable)
- "What is BS 5266?" (emergency lighting)
- "What is a responsible person under RR(FS)O?"
- "How much should FM cost per sqm?"

#### 3. Structured for Featured Snippets
Format requirements:
- Answer question in first 40-60 words
- Use bullet points (Google loves bullets)
- Include relevant schema markup
- Add FAQ sections to all authority content

### CTR Optimization

**Meta Description Formula:**
`[Benefit] + [Authority Signal] + [Urgency] = Click`

Examples:
- ✅ "24/7 engineer access + 98.7% SLA performance. Switch FM provider risk-free."
- ✅ "BS 5839 fire alarm testing schedule + free compliance audit. Avoid prosecution."

**Title Tag Strategy:**
- Lead with primary keyword
- Include year (2025) for freshness signal
- Add power words: "Complete", "Ultimate", "Guide", "Proven"

**Schema Enhancements:**
- Star ratings on case studies (Review schema)
- FAQ schema with speakable markup
- BreadcrumbList on all pages
- Article schema on resources
